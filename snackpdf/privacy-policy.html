Privacy Policy

How we protect your privacy and handle your data

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

No File Storage: SnackPDF processes your PDF files locally in your browser. We do not upload, store, or retain any of your files on our servers.

Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service, including tool usage frequency and general performance metrics.

Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address and encrypted password for authentication purposes only.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Provide and improve our PDF processing services
  • Authenticate user accounts
  • Analyze service usage patterns (anonymously)
  • Communicate important service updates

3. Data Protection

Local Processing: All PDF operations are performed locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device.

No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Security: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any data we do collect.

4. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Under UK GDPR and Scottish law, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Data portability

5. Legal Basis and Jurisdiction

Legal Basis: We process data based on legitimate interests and user consent where required.

Jurisdiction: This privacy policy is governed by Scottish law and UK GDPR. Any disputes will be resolved under the jurisdiction of Scottish courts.

Data Controller: SnackPDF, operated from Scotland, United Kingdom.
